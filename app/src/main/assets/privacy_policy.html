MoneyPulse Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Welcome to MoneyPulse. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 SMS Messages

MoneyPulse requires access to SMS messages specifically for the purpose of automatically detecting and categorizing financial transactions from your bank notifications.

We only process messages that appear to be from financial institutions (banks, credit cards, payment services) and contain transaction information. All other messages are ignored and never processed, stored, or transmitted.

All SMS processing happens entirely on your device. We do not transmit your SMS data to any server or third party.

1.2 Financial Information

MoneyPulse collects the following financial information:

  • Transaction amounts
  • Transaction dates
  • Merchant/payee information
  • Transaction types (credit, debit, payment)
  • Monthly income (manually provided by you)
  • Budget categories and limits (manually set by you)

This information is used solely to provide you with expense tracking, budgeting, and personal financial management features within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To automatically detect and categorize your financial transactions
  • To provide you with expense tracking and budgeting features
  • To generate insights and analytics about your spending habits
  • To allow you to set and manage budget goals

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. We use industry-standard encryption (SQLCipher) to protect your information.

3.2 Security Measures

MoneyPulse implements the following security measures:

  • Data encryption at rest using secure key storage
  • Optional biometric authentication
  • Automatic app locking when not in use
  • Screen capture prevention for sensitive screens
  • No transmission of financial or SMS data to external servers

4. Data Sharing

MoneyPulse does not share your SMS data or financial information with any third parties.

Your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 SMS Permission

You can choose to deny or revoke SMS permissions at any time through your device settings. If you do so, you will need to enter transaction information manually, but all other app features will remain functional.

5.2 Data Deletion

You can delete all your data from the app at any time by using the "Clear All Data" option in the app settings. This will permanently erase all transaction history and user preferences.

5.3 Notification Permissions

MoneyPulse uses notifications to alert you about budget limits and financial insights. You can manage these permissions in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

MoneyPulse is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
